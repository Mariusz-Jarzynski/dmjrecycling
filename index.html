about us

DMJ RECYCLING LTD is a family run company, where we understood how important are customer service, punctuality and diligence. We are collecting scrap metal and cables that would otherwise be thrown away as trash, which then are turning into new products. We offer free collection service from construction and demolition sites, houses etc. We cover the following area: Reading, London, Luton, Gatwick.

sell to us

Do you have scrap metal or cables for sale? Contact us on 07808 262 891 (call, text message, WhatsApp) and we will arrange a convenient time to collect it from your place (construction sites, demolition sites, houses, garages etc). We use very precision scale to weight scrap metal. You can be sure that you will get top prices and the instant payment for your scrap metal.
