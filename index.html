about us

DMJ RECYCLING LTD is a family run company, where we understood how important are customer service, punctuality and diligence.

Our activity is the purchase of scrap metal and cables, that would otherwise be thrown away as trash and end up in a landfill, which then are segregating and turning into new products.

Buying and recycling scrap metal and cables is important for the environmental protection, as it reduces the amount of waste and the consumption of raw materials

Our goal is essentially to take care of the environment and the country's economy by recovering raw materials and reducing waste. We also strive to promote the idea of sustainable development and educate customers about respecting natural resources!

We offer free collection service from construction and demolition sites, houses etc, and cover the following area: Reading, London, Luton, Gatwick.
